Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 cup yeast
  • 1 egg 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter

For the filling:

  • 2/3 dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix combined into a dough.
